---
title: Advertiser Role
description: Definition and intended usage for the Adstract Advertiser role.
---

The `Advertiser` role is for entities that place ads through Adstract.

## What it means

An Advertiser is a party that creates campaigns and supplies ad content intended
for eligible Publisher inventory.

## Relationship to Publisher

Publisher accounts represent inventory where ads can be placed. Advertiser
accounts represent campaign demand intended for that inventory.
