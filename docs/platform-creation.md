Creating a platform is the first step after signing up as a Publisher.
You do **not** need to be verified first — platform creation comes before verification
in the onboarding flow.

## Before you start

- You must have a `Publisher` account. See [Signup](/signup).

## How to create a platform

1. Open the sidebar and go to `Platform`.
2. Click `Create Platform`.
3. Fill in the creation form (see below).
4. Click `Create Platform` to submit.

## Platform creation form

### Basic information

- `Name` (required)
- `URL` (required)
  - `https://` is added automatically if missing.
- `Description` (optional)
- `Type` (required):
  - `web`
  - `mobile`
  - `plugin`
  - `other`
- `Domain Type` (required):
  - `Horizontal`
  - `Vertical`

### How to choose Domain Type

- Choose `Horizontal` if your platform is a general conversational AI application or aggregator with broad usage.
- Choose `Vertical` if your platform is focused on a specific domain (for example, coding), then select the matching vertical type.

### Insights section

The Insights section provides Adstract with data about your platform's audience and traffic characteristics.
Complete all fields in this section accurately — this information is used during verification review.

### Revenue section

The Revenue section provides Adstract with information about your platform's monetisation context.
Complete all fields in this section accurately — this information is also reviewed during verification.

## Making your platform active

After creation, ensure your platform is set to **active**. An inactive platform cannot be used for API
key generation or counted toward verification eligibility.

## Result after creation

After submission, the new platform appears in the `Platform` page list.

## Next steps

- Continue to [Sandbox Key](/sandbox-key) to create your first API key and begin generating traffic.
