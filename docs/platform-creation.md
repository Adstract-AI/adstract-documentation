You can create a platform only after successful Publisher verification.

## Prerequisite

- Your Publisher account must be verified by an administrator.

## How to create a platform

1. Open the sidebar and go to `Platform`.
2. After verification, a `Platform` option is shown.
3. Click `Platform`.
4. On the Platform page, click `Create Platform`.
5. Fill in the creation form.
6. Click `Create Platform` to submit.

## Platform creation form

Complete the following fields:

- `Name` (required)
- `URL` (required)
  - `https://` is added automatically if missing.
- `Description` (optional)
- `Type` (required):
  - `web`
  - `mobile`
  - `plugin`
  - `other`
- `Domain Type` (required):
  - `Horizontal`
  - `Vertical`

## How to choose Domain Type

- Choose `Horizontal` if your platform is a general chatbot/aggregator with broad usage.
- Choose `Vertical` if your platform is focused on a specific domain (for example, coding), then select the matching vertical type.

## Result after creation

After submission, the new platform appears in the `Platform` page list.
