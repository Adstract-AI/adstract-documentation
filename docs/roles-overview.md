---
title: Roles Overview
description: Overview of account roles in the Adstract platform.
---

Adstract defines three role types:

- [User](/role-user): default/neutral account role.
- [Publisher](/role-publisher): role for inventory owners.
- [Advertiser](/role-advertiser): role for ad campaign participants.

If you are integrating Adstract in your product, use the `Publisher` role.
