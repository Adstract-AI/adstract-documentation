title: Beneficiary Setup
description: Configure your beneficiary (payment details) as a prerequisite for Publisher verification.
---

Before you can request verification, you must configure a valid **beneficiary**
on your Publisher account. A beneficiary is the payment recipient that Adstract
will use to process revenue from your Billing API key traffic after verification.

## What is a beneficiary

A beneficiary represents the legal entity or individual that will receive payments
for verified ad traffic generated through your platform.

## Before you start

- You must have a `Publisher` account. See [Signup](/signup).

## How to set up a beneficiary

1. Open your account settings or Publisher profile.
2. Navigate to the **Beneficiary** section.
3. Fill in all required payment and identity fields.
4. Save your beneficiary details.

Adstract requires a valid and complete beneficiary before a verification request
can be submitted. Incomplete or missing beneficiary details will block the
verification request step.

## Important

- Beneficiary details must be accurate. Incorrect information may delay verification
  approval or cause payment issues after approval.
- You can update beneficiary details at any time, but changes may be subject to
  re-review.

## Next steps

- After completing beneficiary setup, continue to [Verification](/verification) to
  request your account review.
