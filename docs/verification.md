---
title: Verification
description: Publisher account verification process after signup.
---

After creating a `Publisher` account, your account enters a verification step.

## Prerequisite

- You must be signed up first.

## What happens next

- Your account is reviewed by an Adstract administrator.
- Verification may take some time depending on review volume.
- Once approved, your Publisher account can continue with the onboarding flow.

## Important

- Verification is required for Publisher onboarding.
- If approval is still pending, wait for the administrator review to complete.

## Next steps

- After your account is verified, continue to [Platform Creation](/platform-creation).
