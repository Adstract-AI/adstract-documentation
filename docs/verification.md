Publisher verification is the process through which Adstract confirms that your
account is eligible for paid production traffic. Verification is **not** automatic
at signup — you must meet a set of requirements before you can request it.

## Verification prerequisites

Before you can request verification, you must complete all of the following:

1. **Active platform** — You must have at least one platform created and in an active state. See [Platform Creation](/platform-creation).
2. **200 unique ad clicks** — You must generate at least 200 unique ad clicks using a [Sandbox API key](/sandbox-key). Sandbox traffic is not paid and exists solely to demonstrate integration quality and traffic eligibility.
3. **Beneficiary setup** — You must have a valid beneficiary (payment details) configured on your account. See [Beneficiary Setup](/beneficiary).

## How to request verification

Once all prerequisites are met:

1. Open your Publisher dashboard.
2. Navigate to the **Verification** section.
3. Click **Request Verification**.
4. Your account enters a pending review state.

## What happens during review

- An Adstract administrator reviews your account, platform, and sandbox traffic data.
- Review time varies depending on volume.
- You will be notified when your verification status changes.

## After approval

Once your verification is approved:

- You can create a **Billing** API key for your platform. See [Billing Key](/billing-key).
- All valid traffic generated with a Billing key is eligible for payment.
- Sandbox keys remain available but traffic from them is never paid.

## Important

- Verification cannot be requested until all three prerequisites above are fully satisfied.
- If your request is still under review, wait for the administrator decision before contacting support.

## Next steps

- After verification is approved, continue to [Billing Key](/billing-key) to create your production API key.
